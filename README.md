# M&T Bank (mandt-bank)
M&T Bank is a regional bank holding company headquartered in Buffalo, New York, providing commercial and retail banking services to consumers and businesses across the eastern United States. As a subsidiary of M&T Bank Corporation (NYSE: MTB), it offers checking, savings, lending, mortgage, wealth management, and treasury services.
